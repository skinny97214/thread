wrap yourself in pixels

DAWN PICKA

Crystal begins each scarf with an image of a person caught in a moment of action. The raw materials of the piece come from a painting by one of the few female Art Deco painters, Tamara Lempicka. Those original pixels were morphed through hundreds of transformations, duplications and remixes. It pays homage to a Surrealists’ sunrise. Or perhaps it’s a Mandelbrot in shimmering color. What do you find in it?

This scarf is 100% silk crepe de chine

130cm x 200 cm
